Personal

What do I do outside of physics?

HPDVideography

In the summer of 2019 I started my freelance photography / videography business HPDVideography. I primarily work with bands creating promotional content. I have worked with a number of local triangle artists including Peter Lamb and the Wolves, Lisa R and the Lucky Stars, The Clothespins, dearxay, and more! I also have experience in corporate marketing and event photography / videography. If you are interested in my servies please do not hesitate to reach out!

Music

I have been playing guitar for almost 13 years and tenor saxophone for 5 years. I have a home music studio in my bedroom and I am constantly working on music in my free time. I will share any new projects here.
